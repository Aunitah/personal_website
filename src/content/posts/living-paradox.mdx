---
title: "Living Paradox"
date: "2024-03-20"
---

Forsaken and cherished.
A heart as pure as snow, yet burdened with guilt,
Brimming with love, yet ice-cold.
Daily I pledge to my soul,
To seek solace amid this paradox.
I hope this time I don't retreat,
I don't flee,
From the love that is rightfully mine.
